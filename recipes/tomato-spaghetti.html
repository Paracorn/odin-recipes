Tomato spaghetti

This scrumptious pasta Napoletana is ready in less than 30 minutes and uses a handful of everyday ingredients. It’s juicy, full of flavor and totally delicious.

It’s also super easy, inexpensive, and you can get a weeknight family meal on the table with a few pantry staples and a little effort!

ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 brown onion, diced
  • 800g crushed tinned tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, lightly packed

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, then add the onion and cook until softened. Then add tinned tomatoes and 1/2 cup water, simmer 5 minutes.
  2. Toss with pasta and cooking water per Base Directions. Toss through basil and serve with parmesan.
