Fiery pasta

Pasta Arrabiata is a classic! It's healthy, easy to make, and the fiery tomato sauce will please all the spice lovers out there.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves , crushed
  • 21 oz / 600g crushed tinned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chilli flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium high heat. Add garlic, stir until fragrant, then add the tinned tomatoes and chilli flakes, simmer on medium for 5 minutes.
  2. Toss with pasta and cooking water per Base Directions. Toss through parsley and serve with parmesan.
